The Wikimedia Commons has images on almost anything you can imagine. While putting together this website, I realized that I had not captured photos of the vehicles I was collecting sayings from. To make the terms tro-tro and tricycle clear, I browsed for those to present some pictures of each. In fact, such pictures also include the sayings, although that was not their original intent.

Another vehicle used for carriage is the okada or motorcycle taxi.
The safety of these vehicles is debated (AMA report) (acebook post),
but they can thread their way through congested streets and narrow passages
better than trotros or tricycles.

While this photo was taken in Togo, similar scenes are common in Ghana.
